How to Make The Perfect Cup Of Coffee

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Description

Making the perfect cup of coffee can be difficult, but after years of practice I have finally figure out how to create a morning up of perfection!

Ingredients

  • 1 Starbucks Keurig K-Cup
  • 1/2 cup 2% milk
  • 1/4 cup half and half

Directions

  1. Place K-Cup in the Keurig machine and press the large cup button
  2. Once coffee is in the cup, pour in the 2% milk and the half and half
  3. Please drink responsibly! Coffee will be hot!
